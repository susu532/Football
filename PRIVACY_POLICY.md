## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide when using the Game:

- **Player Name/Username**: Your chosen display name in the game
- **Team Selection**: Your preferred team (Red or Blue)
- **Character Selection**: Your chosen character type
- **Chat Messages**: Messages you send in the in-game chat
- **Feedback**: Any feedback, support requests, or communications you send us

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Game, we automatically collect certain information:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, browser type
- **Game Data**: Gameplay statistics, scores, match history
- **Connection Data**: IP address, connection quality, ping/latency
- **Usage Data**: Time spent playing, features used, maps played
- **Error Logs**: Crash reports and technical errors

### 2.3 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

- Remember your preferences (character selection, settings)
- Analyze usage patterns
- Improve game performance
